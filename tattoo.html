Read Me

  • I will NOT tattoo other artists' works.
    • Exceptions: artist is deceased or famously in the public domain.
    • Please do not come to me with a photo of another tattooist's work and say "Can you do this" or "I want this exact design."
  • My minimum is $100, with an hourly of $130. However, I do work with budgets so please let me know if you have one in mind.
    • Most of my designs (~6 inches) with a lot of details cost $325–$520, at 2.5–4 hours.
    • My flash designs are always 10% off. I will tattoo each design only once. However, I may be able to draw a variation for a specific flash design if I find appropriate. For available flash, please check my Instagram.
    • Tips are not expected, but always appreciated.
  • I am requiring a deposit of $50 to avoid no-shows:
    • This is non-refundable unless you contact me to cancel or reschedule 1 week prior to your appointment time.
    • I CANNOT lock you down for a date until I receive your deposit.
    • This goes towards your final price.
  • You will receive the final design on the day of your appointment when you come in. I can make minor adjustments at the studio. If there are any drastic changes that need to be made, you will lose your deposit and will have to go through the appointment process again. This is an extremely rare situation and doesn’t happen very often. I ask that you trust in my work and my process.
  • Feel free to bring any entertainment such as movies/shows to watch, books, etc! Your comfort is my #1 priority.
